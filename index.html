Pips are micro-credits for AI features across all apps. The base app is always free — you only pay for AI when you use it.

What are Pips?

Pips are small credits used to power AI features across all Pip apps. Each AI operation uses a fraction of a Pip, keeping costs transparent and fair.

💡
1 Pip ≈ €0.03 of AI compute — a single analysis costs a fraction of a cent
🔑
One license key works across all Pip apps on all your devices
♾️
Credits never expire — use them at your own pace
🔒
Stored securely on your device — never shared with third parties
